SJS, an acronym for SRI JAGANNATH SPINNERS PVT.LTD, and established in the year 1956, is governed by a Council of Administration consisting of member representatives from the Industry, Government and Scientists. SJS is sponsored by the Industry and is supported by the Ministry of Textiles, Government of India.
Sprawling in a campus of about 13.14 hectares, SJS is within easy access of a large number of textile mills. With a floor space of about 15,000 sq. m., SJS houses its well equipped testing, electronics and calibration laboratories, pilot mills, library, etc. SJS has a full range of sophisticated textile testing instruments and modern machines and is one of the best equipped textile research organizations in the World.
